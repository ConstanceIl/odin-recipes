Budín de pan

Description

Budín de pan is a common desert in Argentina. It means 'bread pudding' and is a great -tasty- way of reusing bread leftovers

Ingredients

All you need to make this delicious dessert is:

  • Sugar
  • Bread leftovers
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Lemon zest

Steps

  1. First, make a caramel pouring sugar into a savarin
  2. Chop the bread and mix it with warm milk. You can leave it in chunks or use a mixer to make the mix smoother
  3. Add sugar, vanilla extract and lemon zest to the milk and bread mix
  4. Mix the eggs and pour them into the milk mix
  5. Put the mix in the savarin and take it to a low-middle oven in a water bath for aproximately 50 minutes
  6. Serve when it's cold with some whipped cream and dulce de leche
