Mug cake

Description

A mug-cake is as simple as a cake, made in a mug and cooked in the microwave. Ideal for a quick break between studying sessions or working hours

Ingredients

All you need to make this delicious dessert is:

  • Sugar
  • Self-raising flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the sugar in the mug
  2. Add one egg, milk, oil and vanilla extract and mix it together
  3. Once the mix is smooth, cook it for 2 minutes in the mcirowave
  4. That's it! You can eat ir right away. Be careful tho, because it will be hot!
