Bolognesa sauce

Description

The bolognesa sauce is one of the most delicious sauces you can eat your pasta with.

Ingredients

All you need to make this delicious sauce is:

  • Minced beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and garlic and saute them.
  2. When the onion is soft, add the minced beef with all the condiments of your choice. Move them from time to time to prevent it from losing water.
  3. When the meat is thoroughly cooked, add the tomato extract and some water. Leave it alone at minimum and move it every 20 minutes.
  4. Add the red wine and cook until the alcohol has evaporated.
  5. Serve with your favorite pasta!
